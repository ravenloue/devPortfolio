About Me

  Balancing full-time work with my studies, I recently graduated from the University of the People with a 3.8 GPA—an achievement that I feel reflects both my dedication and resilience. For me, every project feels like a puzzle: finding the right mix of logic, design, and creativity to bring an idea to life.
  Most of my work focuses on full-stack and front-end development, where I enjoy turning complex requirements into clean, intuitive interfaces. I've built projects using both the Spring framework, NodeJS, and Django, which has given me experience working across different stack configurations and the flexibility to adapt to varied development environments, while also working with object-oriented design, learning Agile best practices, and modern web development approaches.
  At the same time, I'm curious about where tech is heading—especially in areas like AI and AR. Exploring these fields keeps me thinking about new possibilities and how digital experiences can evolve. This portfolio is a snapshot of my journey—projects that highlight not only my technical skills but also emphasize the creativity and problem-solving mindset I bring to my work.
